Building Excellence Since 1981

Hallekem Limited is an indigenous based international company owned and managed by Polish Nigerian professionals, operating with over 43 years of combined experience. We engage in all aspects of civil engineering services including design, construction, infrastructure maintenance, housing development, and project consultancy.

Our portfolio spans major infrastructure projects across Nigeria, Poland, Jamaica, and Romania—from bridge rehabilitation and road construction to World Bank-funded erosion control initiatives. We provide turnkey solutions, bid contracts, management contracting, and estate development services.

The ability of Hallekem Limited to deliver quality is driven by our management team's unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team comprises dynamic, self-motivated professionals with proactive approaches to problem-solving. We believe in excellence without compromise, which is why our name stands conspicuously among industry experts. With an annual turnover averaging ₦12-19 billion, we have the financial capacity and technical expertise to execute projects of any scale.

Our Safety Policy

Hallekem Limited is committed to safeguarding the health and safety of all workers. We comply with all applicable safety laws including the Factory Decree No. 16 of 1987 and Mineral Oil Safety Regulations of 1963.

Our Safety Motto: "Health is Wealth"

Safety Provisions

  • Weekly site safety meetings
  • Personal protective equipment (helmets, safety boots, gloves, goggles, rain coats)
  • Specialised safety training (first aid, fire fighting, swimming)
  • Skilled workers only for specialised operations
  • Accident reporting protocols
  • Regular equipment inspection
  • Licensed drivers for all vehicles
